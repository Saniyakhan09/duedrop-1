Benefits of Investing in Plastic Credits

Incorporating plastic credits into plastic reduction and sustainability strategies can significantly contribute to achieving ESG and plastic stewardship objectives. Other benefits are:

▲

For Planet

Scale plastic waste collection to reduce environmental pollution.

▲

For People

Improve livelihoods through dignified work. Enhance health and safety for informal waste sector workers. Foster equity and inclusion by engaging local communities.

▲

Economic Advantage

Ensure proper downstream plastic management. Generate a recycled plastic supply for ambitious content targets. Accelerate the transition to a circular economy for plastics.
